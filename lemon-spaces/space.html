House Rules

General house rules:

“Due to some rules and regulations in Egypt, we need to add some additional rules for your own and our safety

- Copies of ID's / Passports of all coming guests must be sent on a given email upon booking confirmation and prior arrival to guarantee Check-IN

- A rental agreement contract will be sent to sign by you upon booking confirmation. -No visitors are allowed unless we get notified before it

IMPORTANT NOTE:

We are very keen on making sure you have all the items you need to have a comfortable stay. We kindly ask you to make sure to keep all items you found in place before your departure. Any missing items (i.e towels, utensils, keys, furniture items etc) will be considered as a security breach and will be deducted from your security deposit.

For Arab passport holders only:

-For couples, we will require a copy of the official marriage certificate "Orfi" or any unofficial documents are not accepted.

-Breaking any of the above stated house rules will put us in a critical situation with the Egyptian authorities and you will be asked to leave with no refund. Thank you for understanding and cooperating with us”

CANCELLATION POLICY

Guest Cancellation Policies:

- Short Term stays/ up to 1 month - MODERATE policy

Full refund up to 5 days prior to check-in

- Long Term/ 1-3 months - STRICT policy

If the check-in date is at least 14 days away, full refund or cancellation is allowed within 48 hours from the booking payment If the check-in date is at least 7 days away, 50% refund or cancellation is allowed No refunds for cancellations made within 7 days of check-in.

- Long Stay/ 3 months + - SUPER STRICT

Full refund up to 30 days prior to check-in

Cancelling from Lemon Spaces Side:

If a booking was confirmed and guest did not arrive, depends on how strong the reason is. Up to 50% Refund is allowed if a booking was confirmed and guest arrived and any of the house rules were broken then No Refund
