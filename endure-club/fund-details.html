Code of Conduct

FundersClub provided the following information and disclosures.

Do you plan to make investments in the companies that this fund invests in either personally or with another fund?

We do not expect to personally invest directly in companies that are funded by this Accelerate fund outside of FundersClub. We may invest in them with other funds managed by FundersClub, such as other single- or multi-company FundersClub funds. This Accelerate fund specifically targets investing in YC S19 startups that our institutional VC fund, FCVC Fund l, L.P. ("FCVC"), is co-investing in or already invested in. Such other investment funds may have priority access to invest in the YC S19 startups prior to the Accelerate fund, and as a result it may be that some YC S19 startups are in the investment portfolio of such other funds but not in the Accelerate fund. FundersClub Inc. and/or affiliates of FundersClub Inc. and/or employees of FundersClub Inc. may be investors in funds that are investing in the companies that this Accelerate fund is investing in; the terms under which those funds invest in a YC S19 startup may be different than the terms under which the Accelerate fund invests.

Do you plan to invest at the same terms as this fund's investment?

In such scenarios, in a given financing round, we would seek to invest and target investing at the same terms across the various funds that we manage, but we cannot guarantee that every startup will accept investments from all such funds on the same terms, in part due to the rolling timelines of seed round financings.

How will investment amounts in a given company be allocated across funds?

Each fund's position sizing will be decided separately so we will not have ratios applied across funds. FCVC must satisfy its ownership targets before allocations can be made available to this Accelerate fund. As a result, in limited allocation situations, this Accelerate fund may be unable to receive an allocation in certain companies funded by FCVC and/or may receive reduced allocations and/or may invest under different terms than FCVC.

Do you anticipate receiving any advisory shares in the companies in which this fund invests?

No.

Are there situations where you will use other capital instead of this fund's capital to invest in an opportunity?

If yes, please describe. We may invest in companies that are funded by this fund with other funds managed by FundersClub.

Are you putting your own money into the fund or not? If yes, what percent (%) of the fund and/or what amount of capital ($) will you put into the fund?

No.

To the extent that the above answers conflict with the Participation Agreement, the terms of the Participation Agreement will control. Opportunities to invest in this Fund are only offered through the Participation Agreement, which contains a full discussion of the risks and conflicts of investing in the Fund. All investors are encouraged to read the Participation Agreement carefully before investing.
