Accelerate FundersClub XXII is targeting investments in startups in the Summer 2019 batch of Y Combinator ("YC"). The fund is targeting:

- Investments in N5-8 of the highest promise startups in the YC S19 batch - Investment terms equivalent or similar to other co-investors - Investments via equity or convertible notes or SAFEs

The fund will be overseen by Endure Club founders Alex Mittal (YC S12) and Boris Silver (YC S12), on behalf of Endure Club, who other than being alumni of the YC tech accelerator, have advised multiple YC founders, and have a track record of securing highly competitive investments* in past top YC companies.

This fund is targeting investments in N5-8 companies. If you seek more diversification, consider allocating across multiple FC Accelerate funds over time.

This fund targets investing in YC S19 startups that FundersClub's institutional VC fund, FCVC, is co-investing in or has already invested in. Conversely, this fund will not invest in any YC S19 startups that FCVC is not also co-investing in or already invested in.

Recent past Accelerate FC funds had an average management fee of 1.5%/year; this fund has an average management fee of 1.75%/year.
