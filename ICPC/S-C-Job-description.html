UI/UX Designer

WunderLand Group

Apply for job

In today’s diverse business environment, We strongly believe that “There is no one size fits all solutions” Therefore, here at interface, innovation is the passion driving us to create tailor made solutions for each and every business model.

As a team, we believe in each other and together we succeed. We offer a multitude of services that range from but not limited to Technology Consultancy, Web Development, Systems Development, Online Marketing Solutions and Web Development for the mobile platform.

About job

Job description

Our creative corporate client is in need of a UI / UX designer for a 40 hour per week freelance role, onsite in Redmond. In this role you will be working on design projects from concept to completion. Overseeing the the full process from wire frames to visual design execution.

Responsibilities

  • 3+ years of professional experience working on website and mobile
  • previous experience with responsive, native platforms
  • strong communication skills, great in team environments and work
  • To be successful in this role, you should have experience with design software and wireframe tools. If you also have a portfolio of professional design projects that includes work with web/mobile

About job

Job Requirements and Qualifications:

  • Bachelor degree in Applied Arts or Computer Science or related.
  • At least 2+ years experience in UI design, ideally for desktop web mobile applications and mobile apps (tablets and phones).
  • Excellent experience in Adobe Creative Products preferably XD, Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Dealing with (Balsamiq, axure RP or similar) is a plus.
  • Solid understanding of current trends and UI design patterns.
  • Be open to receiving objective criticism and improving upon it.

Skills:

  • Excellent Communication Skills.
  • Customer Oriented.
  • Business Oriented.
  • Time/Task Management.
